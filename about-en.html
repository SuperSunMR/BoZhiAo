About us
Home > About us > About us
JiaXing MAINFILL Packaging Machinery Co., Ltd. is a professional enterprise that develops, produces and sells powder dosing, powder filling and powder packaging equipment. The company has brought together a group of professionals engaged in powder filling industry for nearly 20 years, and a team of mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, sales and after-sales service personnel with rich experience in powder filling industry. Since its establishment, the company has successfully developed auger filler, powder automatic filling machine, powder weighing packaging machine, screw conveyor, ribbon mixer, powder filling assembly equipment and powder engineering supporting equipment.


Our products are widely used in food, pharmaceuticals, health products, condiments, food ingredients, beverages, chemical industry and daily chemical and other fields. Our products are all over the country and exported to overseas countries and regions. Depending on advanced engineering design concepts, rich practical experience, mature technology and innovative staff, our company can provide a complete package of packaging process flow from project design, production development, installation and commissioning, technical training to after-sales service according to the actual needs of customers. The auger filler manufactured by our company is widely used in vertical packaging machine, horizontal packaging machine and bag feeding machine industry for packaging powder products. It has a very high market share and has won many good comments, and has formed a good reputation.


"Quality casting brilliant, integrity leading to success," MAINFILL's staff will take this as the belief, and better cooperate with various industries and fields. Because of their specialty, MAINFILL is willing to work together with friends at home and abroad in the pursuit of excellence to create brilliance!
